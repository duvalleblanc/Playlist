Current Playlist



Mach-Hommy - RICHAXXHATIAN (2024)

My Thoughts

This is currently my album of this year. When I first started the album I was immediately entranced by the intro.

Mach-Hommy has consistently released albums that raise the bar on bars, flow, & rhyme schemes.

The majority of the album being produced by SadhuGold adds to the misty foggy canvas he is rapping over.

The stank face I had through this project was nonstop. The first time I heard the production for GORGON ZOE LAN...Wow

This album is going down in my favorite albums of all time...Might Even be in the top 5 or 10 albums I have ever heard...

The Insnae Features from Roc, YOD, Fahim, Black Thought.. all the way down to the Choir Singing in the Final Track "HOLY___"...

The album is nothing short of perfection.

Roc Marciano - Marciology (2024)

My Thoughts

This album is another work of art from Masterclass Rapper/Producer, Roc Marciano.

The previous collab album with Producer/Rapper "The Alchemist", "The Elephant Man's Bones", in 2023, was

such a dark, menancing, heavy collection of songs that I was interested if Roc would continue wit this sound.

It was a pleasant surprise as going through this project that he went back to his soulful & minimalistic style of production.
